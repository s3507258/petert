Senior Mobile App Developer (Android)


Source - https://www.seek.com.au/job/39504379?type=standard&searchRequestToken=c3837bad-166c-4dc4-a1ce-7ed7ff0b9478

My ideal job would be a senior mobile app developer specifically for Android platforms. What a mobile app developer does is that they create applications designed for the operating system which would be android for me. They specialize in writing code, testing the software, and finds fixes for bugs. A senior mobile developer overlooks all other mobile developers alongside doing what they’re meant to do.

Reason why this is my ideal job

This job appeals to me as most app developers tend to work in a group environment focusing on the same project. I think that working with a bunch of people similar to me would be a fun concept. All of us would voice our ideas and concerns on creating a project that we can share to the world. Being a senior mobile developer means that I would get to overlook all other people’s ideas and the actual project itself. I think that being a senior mobile developer, other people in my trade would look up to me and come to me for help when needed. 

Skills, Qualifications and Experience needed for this job

To actually qualify for a senior android developer, one would have to be someone that has worked in the developer trait for years. The skills required would be different for each company. Each company wants their employees to have a wide knowledge on a language that they choose. Some skills that would be needed are

- Java, android development

- Android Software Development Kit (SDK)

- Extensible Markup Language (XML)

The job would also require experienced workers. They would need at least 3 years minimum experience for a coding language. The qualifications for this position would also to have graduated with a Bachelor of Computer Science or software engineering.
